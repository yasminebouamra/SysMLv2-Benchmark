A SysMLv2 model that focuses on vehicle parts, specifically looking into how different parts like engines and transmissions contribute to the overall mass of a vehicle. The model includes a calculation for summing up the masses of various vehicle parts to calculate the total mass of the vehicle. It utilizes the International System of Quantities for mass values and a numerical function for summation. Additionally, the code showcases the inheritance feature by making a vehicle a specialized type of vehicle part and demonstrates creating instances and aggregating attributes from these instances.
