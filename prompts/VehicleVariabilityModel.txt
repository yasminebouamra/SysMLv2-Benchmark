A vehicle design model that incorporates variability to represent different configurations and features using a structured SysMLv2 approach. The model includes base design elements, such as vehicle parts and actions, with added flexibility to cover multiple product variants. It introduces variation points for parts like engines, transmissions, and sunroofs, alongside actions like torque generation and amplification. Constraints are utilized to ensure viable configurations. The model culminates in defining a specific vehicle instance with selected attributes, showcasing how complex systems can be modularly and scalably designed to facilitate automotive engineering.
